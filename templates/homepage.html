Our Platform's Story

The founder of the ChatGPTmall platform, Benny, has always had a dream of providing product manufacturing capabilities according to customer needs. During the pandemic, the supply chain was disrupted, and his global customers felt helpless as their profitable demands could not be produced or delivered. So he began to think about how to break this predicament and came up with the idea that people can create their own virtual factories and discuss product details with AI and factory members. When more people need it and production capacity is feasible, production will proceed according to plan; otherwise, nothing will be done. This idea had been brewing in his mind for many years, but he had never found like-minded people.

Until ChatGPT came into his life, he began to use this technology and continuously explore and communicate his own experience and ideas. With the support of Microsoft, ZkZeco Technology, and Hong Kong Pannex, he worked together with engineers from Ukraine, the United States, and India to finally launch the virtual factory platform for testing in the last few days of April 2023. He believes that as resonance continues to amplify, the ChatLiao platform will be able to serve many places around the world, help more people turn their ideas into real products, and promote social sustainable development.
